A Natural Farming Programme by NABARD

In order to address the emerging concerns and to ensure long-term sustainability of NRM Programme, NABARD is introducing a programme christened as JIVA - meaning thereby “a living being or entity imbued with a life force” in Sanskrit - as an agroecological transformational approach leveraging the pre-existing social and natural capital in watershed and wadi projects.

JIVA program would be implemented at scale to further evolve the processes of its implementation, innovate on its elements, and learn substantive lessons for generating experience in a programmatic mode. JIVA will adopt “natural farming” as its key approach. Capacity building and capable leadership driven by strong conviction for natural farming are the two pivots for the success of the programme.
